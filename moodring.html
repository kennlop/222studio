Mood Ring

LIVE SITE

Web redesign concept for the Brooklyn bar Mood Ring, borne out of Natalie and Kenneth’s joint love for the location and a desire to create an online presence that reflects their ethereal in-person atmosphere. The site itself functions as a mood ring, with its entire color scheme customizable depending on the user’s mood.
